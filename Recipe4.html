Fish-Fry

About the recipe:

Non-vegetarian recipe full of proteins and nutrients.

Avoid if you have Fish Allergies

Prep-Time: 20-30 mins

Instructions:

1.Clean the pieces of fish throughly.

2.Apply Kokum(Garcinia indica),salt,indian masala and leave to marinate.

3.Take besan (begal gram flour) and add salt and masala according to your taste and mix.

4.Coat the fish in the mix .

5.Take oil in a frying pan enough to fry the fish and heat it

6.Put the coated fish in the pan and fry both sides evenly.

7.Fry until brown.

8.Take the fish out in a plate. Your dish is ready.
