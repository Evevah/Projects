Plain Sandwhich

About the recipe:

A healthy short-time snack

Prep-time:7-10 mins(excluding the time required to boil a potato)

Instructions:

1.Boil a potato and keep it peeled and ready.

2.Take two slices of bread.

3.Apply green chutney,butter(optional) on one side either slice.

4.Chop into thin slices: Tomatoes, cucumber,beetroot,carrot,baked peeled potato.

5.Take on slice of the slices of bread. And arrange the slices of vegetables on the slice of bread.

6.Place the other slice of bread on the top of the so form stack.

7.Cut from middle. Enjoy with tomato sauce/by itself
