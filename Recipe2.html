French-Fries

About the recipe:

Vegetarian snack. High Calory content.

Prep-time:15-20 mins

Instructions:

1.Cut a Potato into sticks(long)

2.Pour oil in a Fryer(if avaliable) or into a large pot/Kadai.

3.Heat the oil.

4.Pour the potato slices into the oil.

5.Fry till golden brown.

6.Sprinkle some salt,pepper(optional),masala(optional) on them.

7.Enjoy with tomato sauce/ranch/by themselves
