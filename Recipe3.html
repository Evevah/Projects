Omelette

About the recipe:

Non-vegetarian type of dish, packed with proteins, and nutrients. High fat value if yolk is included.

Time Required: 10-15 mins

Instructions:

1.Brush a fry pan with oil with the help of some sort of brush. And heat the pan

2.Crack open an egg into a bowl.Seperate the yolk if required.

3. Add a pinch of salt.(Optional: Finely Chopped Chillies/Finely chopped onion.)

4.After the pan is sufficiently heated, pour the contents of the bowl into the pan.

5.Fry it until the the egg solidifies at the base.

6.Taking care that all the liquid part stays within the pan, Flip the egg

7.Fry the flipped side as well.

8.Fry until the entire egg has solidified nicely.

9.Plate the Omelette in a dish/use in a sandwhich and enjoy.
